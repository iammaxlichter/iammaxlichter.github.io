Signet Jewelers

  • Signet Jewelers is the world's largest reatiler of diamond jewelry, acting under the brands of Kay Jewelers, Zales, Jared, James Allen, and so many more. During the Summer of 2023 I had the marvelous opporutnity to take an internship under this luxurious brand. Over 12 weeks, I worked as a quality engineer under the supervision of Sam Ibrahim. My time at Signet was memorable, as the people I met and worked with have been some of the most influential figures i've ever had in my life

  • At Signet, I worked inside the corporate office in Coppell, Texas. Some of the most notable achievments from my internship include... mastering agile methodology through Atlassian Jira by manually and automatically testing hundreds and hundreds of user story test cases for over 8 separate working E-commerce websites, developing an incredibly deep understand of a test case and its whole life cycle, and mastering my comprehension on how an E-Commerce business runs and functions. There is plenty of information I'm forgetting, but those were a few prominent takeaways from my internship.

    From the beginning of my 12 week internship at Signet Jewelers to the end, I learned an crazy volume of information regarding quality assurance/engineering, the software development life cycle, working efficiently through sprints with a giant, extremely intelligent team, and so so so much more.
